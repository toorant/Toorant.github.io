Tooran is currently a Ph.D. candidate (final year) at Université de Sherbrooke, Quebec, Canada. She started her Ph.D. in July 2019. She is working with Prof. Ammar Yahia , Prof. Kamal Khayat, Prof. Jeffrey S. Marshall and Dr. Masoud Hosseinpoor. On July 2019 Tooran carries out research on concrete shows a complex rheological behavior to predict the heterogenous rheological behavior of concrete during pipe flow. Due to the presence of particles with different sizes in paste, concrete shows a complex rheological behavir. She is involved in developing viscosity model corrosponding to the rheological behavior of concrete pipe flow. Her main research topic is to develop CFD-DEM method to predict the hetrogenous behavior of concrete. Her interests include:

  • Rheology of complex fluid
  • Multiphase and Grannular flow modeling
  • Computatioinal fluid dynamics
  • Discrete Elemet method
  • Coupled CFD-DEM method
