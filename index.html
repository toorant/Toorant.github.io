Tooran is currently a Ph.D. candidate (final year) at Université de Sherbrooke, Quebec, Canada. She started her Ph.D. in July 2019. She is working with Prof. Ammar Yahia , Prof. Kamal Khayat, Prof. Jeffrey S. Marshall and Dr. Masoud Hosseinpoor.. Tooran does research in numerical simulation of pumping process of Self-consolidating concrete (SCC), study on characterization of lubrication layer as well as shear-induced particle migration phenomena during pumping process. Due to the presence of particles with different sizes in paste, concrete shows a complex rheological behavir. She is involved in developing viscosity model corrosponding to the rheological behavior of concrete pipe flow. Her main research topic is to develop CFD-DEM method to predict the hetrogenous behavior of concrete. Her interests include:

  • Multiphase flow
  • Grannular flow
  • Computatioinal fluid dynamic
  • Discrete Elemet method
  • Rheology of complex fluid
